UX Research Project

Research conducted on user experience with the home rental service Marriott

Introduction

At the tail-end of my journey with The Global Tech Experience, I was tasked with completing a simulated project focused on user research on the leading whole-home rental service Marriott Bonvoy Hotels & Villas. This project was designed in collaboration with Publicus Sapient to replicate the development of digital products. Through thorough market research and user interviews, I gained valuable insights into user needs, pain points, and areas for improvement. This experience provided valuable exposure to real-world research methodologies and strategic analysis, despite its simulated nature.
